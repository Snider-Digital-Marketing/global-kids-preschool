Reggio Emilia Philosophy

The Reggio approach puts children at the center of their own learning with teachers as guides. We partner closely with families and the community to support the whole child.

  • Emergent, project-based curriculum
  • Beautiful, purposeful classroom environments
  • Documentation of learning stories
  • Strong family partnerships

Learn more on our current site’s article about the approach.
